Terra is a website that aims to spread awareness about the current environmental issues that surround our planet and teach ways on how to take part in eliminating them. Recently, scientists have been warning us about how our global temperature can increase by 3.2 degrees Celsius in 2100 if we don’t act on our global carbon emissions by 2025. This issue alone tells us the crucial need for global awareness about our environment. Awareness leads to learning, learning leads to action, and action leads to change. By spreading awareness, this website helps in sparking the desire to act and make a change among other people.

According to NASA, the hole in the ozone layer as of the moment is 26.4 million square kilometers. Its size has been the largest since 2015. In an article by Georgette Kilgore in 8billiontrees.com, she mentioned that more than 15 billion tress are cut annually due to deforestation. This value is exceedingly greater than the number of trees planted annually, which is 1.9 billion. According to animalssurroundtheglobe.com, 41,415 plants and animals around the world are on the IUCN Red List of Threatened Species. Approximately 40% of the species on this list are now close to extinction. As reported by Gloria Dickie in her Reuters article, the number of annual deaths caused by water and air pollution since 2015 is 9 million.
